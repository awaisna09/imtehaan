Questions paper

Question 1 [8 marks]

Explain how the following external factors might affect a small retail business:

  • a) Changes in interest rates
  • b) Increased competition from online retailers

Question 2 [6 marks]

Identify and explain three benefits of market research to a business planning to launch a new product.

Question 3 [10 marks]

A manufacturing company is considering changing from batch production to flow production.

a) Explain two advantages and two disadvantages of flow production for this business. [6]

b) Do you think this would be a good decision for the business? Justify your answer. [4]

Question 4 [12 marks]

Read the following case study and answer the questions below:

ABC Electronics is a medium-sized business that manufactures smartphones. The company is facing increased competition and rising costs. The management is considering either reducing prices or increasing advertising spending to maintain market share.

a) Explain two possible advantages and two possible disadvantages of reducing prices. [8]

b) Recommend whether the business should reduce prices or increase advertising. Justify your answer. [4]

Question 5 [8 marks]

Explain four ways in which good customer service can benefit a retail business.

Question 6 [10 marks]

A business is planning to expand by opening new branches in different locations.

a) Identify and explain two sources of finance that would be suitable for this expansion. [6]

b) Explain two factors the business should consider when choosing locations for the new branches. [4]

Question 7 [6 marks]

Explain three motivational theories that managers could use to improve employee productivity.

Question 8 [8 marks]

Discuss how changes in economic factors might affect the success of a luxury car manufacturer.

Answer Guidelines

How to Answer Past Paper Questions:

  • Read each question carefully and understand what is being asked
  • Plan your time according to the marks allocated
  • Use business terminology appropriately
  • Support your answers with relevant examples
  • Structure your responses clearly
  • Review your answers before submitting
  • Make sure to answer all parts of each question

Time Management:

  • 2 marks question: 2-3 minutes
  • 4 marks question: 5-6 minutes
  • 6 marks question: 8-10 minutes
  • 8 marks question: 12-15 minutes
